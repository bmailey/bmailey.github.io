Web Hosting

Blue Host

Blue host offers the website hosting plans out of all of the websites I have visited.

    -Their four plans range in price from 2.95$ and month to $13.95 a month
    -Their pro plan is 13.95 with the promotion they are currently running. It is usually 28.99$ a month which is a little more expensive compared to other host website plans I have looked at.
    -Although they do offer the first year of your domain for free.

Their online experts will design your website for you and listen to any feedback you have about its appearance. They are able to listen to people's Ideas and set their website up for success. Their plans cover websites with backup security even though they are prepared for becoming high traffic websites.

The reason I believe that blue host is the best hosting platform is because of their reliability. They are highly rated and have easy to use tools. They also have customer support 24/7 that would be able to answer any questions or fix any problems my website may be experiencing. It is comforting to know that they take good care of their customers, which is worth the expensive cost to me.
