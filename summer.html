My personal favorite, summer. Summer is perfect for outdoor activities. With the warm weather and school being out it is perfect for adventure. For example, hiking, swimming, boating, fishing tanning and much more.

I ussaly spend this day around a lake with my family. We will go boating, ride jet skis and have a BBQ. We then end the night with a firework show.
