"Falling leaves, cozy evenings"

Fall has the first holiday of the seasons. This holiday is Halloween. Throughout the month off fall people will decoate with, pumkins, skeletons, graves and spooky things. THis is all preparing for october 31st.

On october 31st kids will dress up in costumes of any theme. Then they go house to house trick or treating. People will hand out candy to them. This is fun for both adults and children. Preparing for this day people with carve pumkins, watch halloween movies and bake goods.
